About Us

Crunchy, munchy, lovely, airy, lustrous, traditional & authentic Indian-style Sweets & Snacks that keeps your energy levels high in the evenings as well as mornins are readily available at Akshayee Snacks and Sweets shop for you to order with a push of a button! Make your evenings lively, spirited and enthusiastic for your family, kids, friends, and guests who visit you with our stack of wholesome snacks like Chocolate ladoo, kaju katali, bread rasmalai, harabhara kabab, cheesy balls, and all the snacks and sweets in different variants.

Bringing Back the Magic of Mithai

Like bees to honey, we followed the sugar trail across India, discovering recipes, imbibing traditions and mastering new skills to build our very own sweet shop.

Celebrating the Diversity of Indian Snacks

We take familiar snacks that we love and create something entirely new. So take a piece, a slice or whole boxes of your favourites and savour some old fashioned taste..
